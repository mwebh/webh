Personalised QR Codes

What is a QR code?

A QR code is a specially design barcode you can scan with your smartphone camera to perform many different tasks. You could have a QR code to have guests automatically join your WiFi, send people to your website or even put your company contact details directly into someone's contact list so they always have your information to hand.
