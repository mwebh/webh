I have a variety of IT experience across many sectors from healthcare to technology organisations so no matter what you need, you're in good hands.

My background

Since the start of 2020, like many others I've been working from home. I've been lucky to work in the tech industry for a long time so the working from home adjustment didn't take long for me.
I used the additional time I was not spending travelling to work to build my skills in all areas as it has always been my ambition to start a business of my own.

My first IT job was in the NHS where I was part of a technical helpdesk. Taking calls from healthcare staff to help resolve their technical issues is where my passion for IT started. From there I entered a new role in the "deployment" team where we carried out installations of IT equipment across the county.
After the NHS was a big jump for me as I started working for Dyson in the Malmesbury office. This was very different from the environment I was used to and unfortunately due to large scale staff redundancy my journey there didn't last long.

In 2019 I started working for a local company called Cristie Data. Cristie is what taught me most of the networking and infrastructure knowledge I know today. I was exposed to all elements of IT infrastructure like networking, firewalls, storage and cloud data.
2021 came along and I made a move back to healthcare, I started working for a company called Healios. Everything I saw in Healios is wildly different to everything I had experienced previously. Healios is completely remote, hardly anyone works from an office and everything is run from the cloud which is so refreshing to see. While working for Healios I was able to build on my web development skills and learn so many new things that have helped me get to where I am today.
